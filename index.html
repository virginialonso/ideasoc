WHO WE ARE

IDEA Soc is the UCLU Society for International Development and Emerging economic Advance.

We provide any globally-aware UCL student with the opportunity to more fully understand, and meaningfully partake in and influence the development of some of the most interesting countries in the world, directly changing lives through hands-on business engagement with such countries.

For the enterprising and the pioneering, for the curious, and for those who want to revolutionise rather than just change - for anyone who wants to improve lives, read through our objectives.

Make your time at UCL meaningful and permanently memorable. Be part of something special. Improve lives. All are welcome!
